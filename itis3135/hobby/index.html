How To Get Started

How do I get started?

Starting at the gym can be intimidating. Here’s my beginner advice:

  • Start with simple exercises and light weights.
  • Let the enorphins take over.
  • Focus on form over lifting heavy.
  • Stick to a routine that best suits you, and gradually increase difficulty in that routine.
  • HAVE NO QUIT!!!
